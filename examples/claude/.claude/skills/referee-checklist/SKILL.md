# Referee Checklist

1. Confirm the manuscript is the user's own work, public, or approved for AI review.
2. Identify paper type and target field.
3. Summarize in 5–7 sentences.
4. Check text/table/equation consistency.
5. **Identification:** estimand, design (DiD/IV/RD), assumptions, pre-trends, spillovers.
6. **Econometrics:** clustering, functional form, multiple hypotheses.
7. Major issues with evidence from the draft only.
8. Robustness suggestions tied to specific threats.
9. Flag unverifiable citations.
10. Output using `templates/referee_report.md`.
